About Us

The founders of L.T. Services, Inc. created the company in 1982 with one simple objective: to provide high-quality and flexible management at reasonable costs to commercial and government organizations. This objective has shaped our corporate culture and decision-making for over 20 years.

To ensure high-quality services, we employ staff who are highly trained in various areas. We provide continuous training, profit-sharing incentives, and expert supervision to ensure outstanding performance.
