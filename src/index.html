About LTHQ2

L.T. Services Inc. offers complete janitorial services to commercial and government facilities. Founded in 1982, L.T. Services is based in Falls Church, Virginia. We are a stable, local, owner-operated, and professional company with over 25 years of experience and a leader in the industry. We staff our management team with the brightest and best within the industry. Our management approach is direct and effective. The key to this is communication: both internally and externally. This has been our core value from the beginning and will continue to serve our future success.

Combining experience and management with a dynamic workforce, L.T. Services Inc. is the preferred choice of complete building service. We take pride in our most basic principle - superior customer service - while continuing to build upon this concept to develop a trusting long-term relationship with our clients.

At L.T., it is our culture to provide services and benefits that are second to none: financial strength, customized service packages, advanced cleaning techniques, on-going training and compliance with all OSHA, EPA and federal security requirements.
